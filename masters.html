European Master in Software Engineering

In August 2019, I completed a Master in Computer Science and Software Engineering through the EMSE program .

I was ranked first on acceptance (on 1000 candidates) and received a scholarship valued at € 43,000 for 2 years of study. I completed course work in

  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • Data Visualization
  • Statistical Methods
  • Verification & Validation

In this time, I did a 8-month full time internship with Siemens in full stack development. I also had a 2 year research graduate position with DFKI .

I completed my Master Thesis with Fraunhofer Institute for Experimental Software Engineering . My thesis was titled, "Refining and Evaluating a Data-Driven Model Uncertainty Framework with a Traffic Sign Recognition (TSR) Case".
